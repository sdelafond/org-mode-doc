Emphasis and monospace

Emphasis and monospace

You can make words *bold*, /italic/, _underlined_, =verbatim= and ~code~, and, if you must, ‘+strike-through+’. Text in the code and verbatim string is not processed for Org-mode specific syntax, it is exported verbatim. To insert a horizontal rules, use a line consisting of only dashes, and at least 5 of them.
